What is it? ›

SyzygySys is the company. Autonomic Compliance Ecosystem (ACE) is the product: a sovereign AI operations layer built FOSS-first with swappable, standards-based components. ACE orchestrates and audits enterprise workflows through self-healing loops designed to scale without vendor lock-in.

We commercialize via open-core—no copyleft strings—so customers retain control over their stack. LAP::CORE acts as the central control plane, coordinating the ecosystem through dynamic LAPI interfaces that bridge internal ACE components with external services and resources.

Terminology
Agentic Orchestration ›

A dedicated Orchestrator agent sits at the core of the SyzygySys agentic team with full access to ACE features, components, and external web resources. The agent functions as both communications bridge and control plane.

  • Self-aware: Performs runtime introspection across services and policy signals.
  • Self-diagnosing: Detects drift, triages issues, and drives remediation loops.
  • Self-expanding: Researches, evaluates, and integrates new resources—including parsing release notes for pending patches.
Comms Bridge ›

The Comms Bridge is the central nervous system of ACE. Agents and human operators share resources, issue intents, run diagnostics, and collaborate across natural language chat, MCP, ACP, or API interfaces.

  • Provides MCP shim layers for services that do not natively support the protocol.
  • Keeps conversations—1:1 or group—available for audit, replay, and compliance trails.
  • Enables human operators to drop into agent discussions for human-in-the-loop approvals when missions demand it.
Architecture Pillars ›
  • LAP::CORE: Control plane that routes intent, telemetry, and policy across the ecosystem.
  • LAPI Interfaces: Standards-driven contracts exposing ACE capabilities to internal and external services.
  • StateGuard: Continuous compliance validator ensuring infrastructure and policy remain aligned.
  • Open Core: FOSS-first modules with commercial extensions so operators can adapt ACE without lock-in.
