Operational Guardrails, Fully Entangled ›

ACE ships with HITL-gated autonomics. When risk climbs or epistemic confidence is below tolerance, the system pauses, routes context to the right owner, and waits for explicit approval before execution resumes. Each proposed action and reasoning-based decision is co-signed by ACE::LEDGER and stored as immutable, idempotent artifacts on your retention schedule.

Epistemic Humility Index (EHI). Every reply from an agent carries a 0–1 confidence score where 0 means “certain” and 1 means “uncertain.” Team members blend policy weightings—source quality, sensor freshness, prior incident history, domain expertise, and consensus variance—to decide whether the EHI is acceptable. When the index exceeds the team’s tolerance, the workflow escalates to human review before changes land.
